Arduino Setup Guide

  • Upload your sketch to Arduino — it must call Serial.begin(115200) (or match the baud rate above)
  • In your loop, print the moisture value: Serial.println(sensorValue);
  • Supported formats: 523  |  Moisture: 523  |  moisture=45.3%  |  {"moisture":523}
  • Values 0–1023 (raw analog) are auto-detected and converted to %. Values with a % sign are used directly.
  • Close Arduino IDE Serial Monitor before connecting here — only one app can hold the serial port at a time.
  • Click CONNECT ARDUINO, select your COM/ttyUSB port, and data will stream live.
